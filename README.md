Title:
The Prayer Room: Daily Devotional App

This is the official daily devotional app for The Prayer Room 10-Week Intercessor Training.
Built and designed by Oluchi Adebanjo.
This app contains 10 weeks of daily Bible readings, prayer exercises, closing prayer points and memory verses covering the following themes:
Week 1: Foundation of Prayer
Week 2: The Word and Prayer
Week 3: The Holy Spirit and Prayer
Week 4: Holy Ghost Baptism
Week 5: Speaking in Tongues
Week 6: Intercession
Week 7: Spiritual Warfare
Week 8: Fasting and Prayer
Week 9: Prophetic Prayer
Week 10: Fire Brand Intercessors, Commissioned
Members can navigate to any week and day, mark their progress, and write personal journal notes. Progress is saved automatically on their device.
